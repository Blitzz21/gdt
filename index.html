FAQ

How do I send photos? +

Use the secure uploader at checkout. iPhone and Android friendly. You'll get instant confirmation once our design desk receives them.

Can I approve before printing? +

Yes. We send a mobile-ready proof within six business hours. Reply with tweaks and we adjust within the same day.

Do prints last through seasons? +

Polaris inks sink into the fibers—no vinyl layer to crack. We've washed samples 60+ times without fade.

What about whole teams? +

We assign you a project manager, sync on roster names, and stage all proofs for a single approval. Bulk pricing triggers automatically.
